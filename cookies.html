What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies can be "first-party" (set by the website you visit) or "third-party" (set by external services). They can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain until they expire or you delete them).

We use cookies to ensure the platform functions correctly, personalise your experience, and understand how our service is used so we can improve it.

How to Disable Cookies in Your Browser
You can manage or disable cookies directly through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will affect site functionality.
  1. Click the ⋮ (three dots) menu in the top-right corner of Chrome
  2. Select Settings
  3. Click Privacy and security in the left sidebar
  4. Select Cookies and other site data
  5. Choose your preferred option: "Allow all cookies", "Block third-party cookies in Incognito", "Block third-party cookies", or "Block all cookies"
  6. To manage cookies for a specific site, scroll down to "Sites that can always use cookies" or "Sites that can never use cookies"
  1. Click the ☰ (hamburger) menu in the top-right corner of Firefox
  2. Select Settings
  3. Click Privacy & Security in the left panel
  4. Under "Enhanced Tracking Protection", choose "Standard", "Strict", or "Custom"
  5. For custom control, select Custom and configure cookies, trackers, and fingerprinters separately
  6. To delete existing cookies, scroll down to "Cookies and Site Data" and click Clear Data
  1. Click Safari in the top menu bar and select Settings (or Preferences on older versions)
  2. Click the Privacy tab
  3. Under "Cookies and website data", choose "Block all cookies" or "Prevent cross-site tracking"
  4. On iPhone/iPad: Go to Settings → Safari → scroll to "Privacy & Security"
  5. Toggle on "Prevent Cross-Site Tracking" and "Block All Cookies" as desired
  6. To remove existing cookies, click Manage Website Data and remove as needed
  1. Click the ⋯ (three dots) menu in the top-right corner of Edge
  2. Select Settings
  3. Click Cookies and site permissions in the left sidebar
  4. Click Cookies and site data
  5. Toggle "Block third-party cookies" on or off as desired
  6. To manage cookies for specific sites, scroll down to "Allow" or "Block" sections and add site-specific rules

Our Cookie Consent Banner
How we obtain and record your consent

When you first visit TravelWise, you will see a cookie consent banner at the bottom of the page. This banner explains the types of cookies we use and gives you the option to:

  • Accept all cookies — enables essential, analytics, marketing, and preference cookies
  • Reject non-essential — enables only essential cookies required for the site to function
  • Customise preferences — opens the cookie manager to choose individual categories

Your consent choice is stored in a cookie (tw_cookie_consent) and honoured for 12 months. You can update your preferences at any time using the Cookie Manager on this page or via this link.
