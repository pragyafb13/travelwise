The Basics
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to website owners about how users interact with their site.

Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. They are uniquely assigned to your device and can only be read by the web server that placed them. TravelWise uses cookies responsibly and transparently — this page explains exactly what we use and why.

How We Use Cookies
Purpose of Cookies on TravelWise

We use cookies across the TravelWise platform for the following core purposes:

  • Authentication & Security — Keeping you securely logged in and protecting against session hijacking and CSRF attacks
  • Booking Flow — Maintaining your search results, passenger details, and payment state through the multi-step checkout process
  • Personalization — Remembering your preferences so TravelWise feels tailored to you on every visit
  • Performance Analytics — Understanding which features are used most and identifying friction points to improve the platform
  • Marketing Effectiveness — Measuring which campaigns and channels drive the most valuable traffic, so we can offer better deals
Your Control
Managing Cookies

Beyond the preference toggles above, you can control cookies directly through your web browser. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies stored on your device
  • Block third-party cookies from being set
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is about to be placed
  • Browse in private/incognito mode, which discards cookies when you close the window

Browser-specific instructions: Chrome  ·  Firefox  ·  Safari  ·  Edge

Note: Disabling essential cookies will prevent core TravelWise features (login, checkout) from functioning correctly.

Third-Party Cookies
Third-Party Cookies on TravelWise

Some cookies on TravelWise are placed by third-party services we use to enhance functionality and measure performance. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies:

  • Google Analytics — Web traffic analysis. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Facebook (Meta) Pixel — Advertising attribution and retargeting. Governed by Meta's Cookie Policy.
  • Google Ads — Ad conversion tracking. Governed by Google's Advertising Data Policy.
  • Razorpay / Stripe — Payment processing. These cookies are essential and required to complete transactions securely.

TravelWise does not control the cookies placed by these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies directly.
