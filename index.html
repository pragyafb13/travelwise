Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my flight booking? +
Go to My Trips → Select your booking → Click Manage → Cancel Booking. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
When will I receive my refund? +
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days for credit/debit cards, 3-5 days for UPI, and instantly for TravelWise Wallet.
How do I change my flight date? +
From My Trips, select your booking and click 'Change Date'. Date change fees and fare differences apply. Free changes on select fare types.
Payment deducted but booking not confirmed? +
Don't worry! In rare cases the amount is automatically refunded within 3-5 business days. Contact our 24/7 support with your transaction ID.
How do I add checked baggage? +
Additional baggage can be added during checkout or post-booking from My Trips. Prices are lower when added during booking.
Still need help?
Our support team is available 24/7
