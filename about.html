Our Mission
Travel should be the birthright of every person who dreams of the world beyond their horizon.

We started TravelWise in 2019 with a simple belief: booking a trip should be as joyful as taking one. We've spent five years building technology that removes the friction — the endless tabs, the confusing pricing, the anxiety of "did I get the best deal?" — so you can focus entirely on the adventure ahead. Today, over 2 million travellers trust TravelWise to take them where they want to go. And we're just getting started.

Our Journey

Five Years of Building the Future of Travel

From a two-person team to a 250-person company that helps millions travel smarter.

2019

TravelWise is Founded

Aryan and Sneha quit their jobs and start building TravelWise in a Bengaluru co-working space. First ₹25 lakh raised from angel investors in September.

2020

The COVID Pivot

The pandemic halts travel globally. TravelWise pivots quickly to offer no-cancellation-fee policies and free rescheduling. This transparency earns 50,000 loyal users who remember us when travel opens up.

2021

₹50 Crore Series A

Raised ₹50Cr in Series A funding led by Lightspeed India. Launched holiday packages, train booking, and the TravelWise mobile app. Team grows to 80 people.

2022

1 Million Users

Hit the 1M active user milestone in March 2022. Launched AI-powered trip planning, earning the Economic Times 'Startup of the Year' recognition. Series B follows at ₹200Cr.

2023

International Expansion

Opened offices in Singapore and Dubai. Launched international flight booking covering 50+ countries. Crossed ₹500Cr GMV. Named one of India's 50 best workplaces.

2026

2 Million Strong & Growing

2M+ travellers, 10,000+ hotel partners, 50+ destination countries. Launched TravelWise Pro for frequent business travellers. Preparing for IPO in late 2026.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from product features to customer support.

🤝

Trust First

We never hide fees, never manipulate prices, and never exploit urgency to push bookings. Every interaction is built on transparency and honesty — even when it costs us a sale.

⚡

Innovation Always

We ship fast, learn faster, and refuse to accept "that's just how it's done." Our engineering culture rewards creative problem-solving over safe incremental improvements.

🌿

Sustainable by Design

We partner only with eco-certified properties, offset all platform emissions, and actively promote slow, conscious travel. Responsible tourism isn't a marketing message — it's a commitment.

🌏

Community-Powered

Our best features come from our users. We have a 10,000-member beta community that shapes the product roadmap. Travellers aren't just customers — they're co-creators.

🏆

Excellence in Execution

A 2-minute query resolution time. A 99.97% platform uptime. A ₹500 guarantee if we're not the best price. We set ambitious standards and hold ourselves accountable to them daily.

🧭

Adventure in Spirit

We hire curious people who take risks, embrace the unexpected, and believe the best journeys are the ones that change how you see the world. This curiosity permeates everything we build.
