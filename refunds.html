Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about refunds and cancellations
Can I cancel a non-refundable ticket?
▼
Non-refundable tickets generally cannot be refunded, but you may still be eligible for a refund of airport taxes and fuel surcharges. In case of airline-initiated cancellation or significant schedule changes (more than 3 hours), you are entitled to a full refund regardless of fare type. Contact our support team if your flight was cancelled by the airline.
What happens if the airline cancels my flight?
▼
If an airline cancels your flight, you are entitled to a full refund of the ticket price, including all fees and taxes. TravelWise will process the refund automatically within 5–7 business days once the airline confirms the cancellation. You may also be offered an alternate flight or travel credit as options.
Can I get a refund to a different account?
▼
Refunds are processed to the original payment method used during booking. This is a security requirement and cannot be changed. If your card has expired, the refund will still be credited to the linked bank account. For wallets, the refund goes back to the same wallet. Contact your bank if you face any issues receiving the amount.
Is the TravelWise convenience fee refundable?
▼
The TravelWise platform convenience fee is non-refundable in most cases. However, if the cancellation is due to an airline cancellation, significant schedule change, or a natural disaster/force majeure situation, the convenience fee may be waived. Please contact our support team for case-by-case evaluation.
How do I cancel a hotel booking that is non-refundable?
▼
Non-refundable hotel bookings cannot be cancelled for a refund. However, in exceptional circumstances (medical emergencies, natural disasters, visa denials), you can contact the hotel directly to request a goodwill waiver. TravelWise can assist you in facilitating this communication with the hotel. Always purchase travel insurance to protect against such situations.
What is the TravelWise Wallet and can I get my refund there?
▼
The TravelWise Wallet is a stored-value account within the app. If you choose to receive your refund to the TravelWise Wallet, it is credited instantly — making it the fastest refund option. Wallet credits can be used for future bookings on the platform. You can request wallet refund as an alternative to the standard bank/card refund from the cancellation screen.
How long does it take for a UPI refund?
▼
UPI refunds are typically processed within 2–5 business days. Once TravelWise initiates the refund, it is sent to the NPCI system which routes it to your bank. The actual credit time depends on your bank's processing. Most UPI refunds are credited within 3 business days. You'll receive an SMS notification when the refund is initiated.
My refund timeline has passed but I haven't received money. What to do?
▼
If the stated refund timeline has passed, please follow these steps: (1) Check the refund status using the checker above; (2) Contact your bank with the TravelWise refund reference number; (3) If the bank cannot trace it, contact TravelWise support at refunds@travelwise.com with your booking reference. We typically resolve such cases within 48 hours of escalation.

Need help with a refund?

Our dedicated refunds team is available 24/7 to assist you with cancellations, refund disputes, and tracking.
