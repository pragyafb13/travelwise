Cancellation & Refund Policies
Refund percentages vary by booking type and how far in advance you cancel
✈️

Flight Cancellations

More than 7 days before 90% refund
2–7 days before 70% refund
Less than 24 hours 50% refund
No-show 0% refund
🏨

Hotel Cancellations

More than 48 hrs before Free cancellation
Within 24 hrs of check-in 1 night charge
No-show Full charge
🗺️

Holiday Packages

30+ days before 80% refund
15–30 days before 50% refund
Less than 15 days 25% refund
Less than 7 days No refund
How to Cancel Your Booking
Three simple steps to initiate a cancellation and get your refund
1
🗂️

Find Your Booking

Go to My Trips, find the booking you want to cancel, and click "Cancel".

2
🔍

Review Charges

Review the cancellation charges and refund amount shown on the confirmation screen.

3
✅

Confirm & Done

Click Confirm. Refund is automatically initiated to your original payment method.
