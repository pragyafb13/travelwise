Flights FAQs

5 questions
How do I book a flight on TravelWise?
▼
Enter your departure city, destination, travel dates, and passenger count on the Flights page. Browse results, compare prices, select your flight, and proceed to checkout. Pay using cards, UPI, net banking, or EMI. Your e-ticket arrives in your email within minutes of payment confirmation.
  • Log in for faster checkout and loyalty points
  • Use filters to sort by price, stops, or airline
  • Add baggage or meals at checkout
Can I change my flight date after booking?
▼
Yes. Most airlines allow date changes subject to availability and applicable change fees. To reschedule:
  • Go to My Trips and click Modify Booking
  • Select a new date and confirm seat availability
  • Pay any fare difference and change fees
Date changes are not permitted within 12 hours of departure on most airlines. Discounted fares may be non-changeable — check your ticket's fare rules.
What is the cancellation policy?
▼
Cancellation charges vary by airline and how far in advance you cancel:
  • More than 7 days before: 90% refund
  • 2–7 days before: 70% refund
  • Less than 24 hours: 50% refund
  • No-show: 0% refund
To cancel, go to My Trips → Cancel Booking. Refunds are processed to your original payment method.
How long does a flight refund take?
▼
Refund timelines depend on your payment method:
  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–7 business days
  • UPI / Net Banking: 2–3 business days
  • TravelWise Wallet: Instant
  • EMI: 7–10 business days
You'll receive an SMS and email notification when the refund is initiated. Track status on the Refunds page.
Why was my payment declined?
▼
Common reasons for payment failure:
  • Insufficient funds or card limit exceeded
  • Incorrect card number, expiry, or CVV
  • Bank security block on online transactions
  • OTP expired or not entered in time
  • Browser session timeout
If money was deducted but booking was not confirmed, it will be auto-refunded within 5–7 business days. Contact support with your transaction ID for faster resolution.

Hotels FAQs

5 questions
How do I confirm my hotel booking?
▼
Once payment is successful, your hotel booking is instantly confirmed. You'll receive a booking confirmation email with a unique booking ID, hotel address, and check-in instructions. You can also access your booking details anytime from My Trips. Show the confirmation QR code or booking ID at the hotel front desk during check-in.
What is the check-in / check-out time?
▼
Standard hotel check-in is 2:00 PM and check-out is 12:00 PM (noon). These times vary by property. Early check-in (before 2 PM) and late check-out (after 12 PM) are subject to availability — contact the hotel 24 hours in advance or use the Special Requests section in My Trips.
Can I modify my hotel reservation?
▼
Yes, subject to the hotel's availability and rate type. Go to My Trips → Modify Booking to change dates, room type, or number of guests. Modifications may involve a fare difference. Non-refundable rate bookings typically cannot be modified — check your booking's fare terms before confirming.
Is breakfast included in my hotel stay?
▼
Whether breakfast is included depends on the rate plan you booked. The meal inclusion is clearly shown on the booking confirmation and during the checkout process. Common plans:
  • Room Only (EP): No meals included
  • Bed & Breakfast (BB): Breakfast included
  • Half Board (MAP): Breakfast + dinner
  • Full Board (AP): All three meals
How do I request early check-in?
▼
To request early check-in, go to My Trips, select your hotel booking, and click Special Requests. You can also call the hotel directly 24–48 hours before arrival. Early check-in is subject to room availability and may attract an additional charge of one-quarter to half a night's rate. TravelWise Elite members often get complimentary early check-in at partner properties.

Payments & Refunds FAQs

5 questions
What payment methods are accepted?
▼
TravelWise accepts a wide range of payment options:
  • Cards: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, RuPay (credit & debit)
  • UPI: GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, any UPI app
  • Net Banking: All major Indian banks
  • Wallets: Paytm, Amazon Pay, MobiKwik
  • EMI: 15+ banks, interest-free options available
  • Buy Now Pay Later: LazyPay, Simpl
My payment failed but money was deducted — what now?
▼
Don't worry — this is a common occurrence during high-traffic periods. The deducted amount will be automatically reversed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. If it doesn't reverse:
  • Check your bank statement for pending/hold transactions
  • Contact your bank with the transaction reference
  • Raise a support ticket with your transaction ID at contact us
We resolve such cases within 48 hours of escalation.
How long do refunds take?
▼
Refund timelines by payment method:
  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–7 business days
  • UPI / Net Banking: 2–3 business days
  • TravelWise Wallet: Instant
  • EMI: 7–10 business days
Track your refund status anytime on the Refunds & Cancellations page.
Can I pay in EMI?
▼
Yes! EMI is available on bookings above ₹5,000. Select EMI at checkout, choose your bank and tenure (3, 6, 9, or 12 months). Supported banks include HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank, and IndusInd. No-cost EMI is available on select offers. EMI is available on credit cards only.
Is my payment data secure?
▼
Absolutely. TravelWise is PCI-DSS compliant and uses 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions. We do not store your full card details — payments are processed through RBI-approved payment gateways. You can also pay via UPI or net banking for zero card data exposure. Enable 2FA on your TravelWise account for additional security.
