Flights

5 questions
How do I book a flight on TravelWise?
▼
Booking a flight is simple. Enter your departure city, destination, travel dates, and number of passengers on the home page or the Flights page. Browse the results, compare prices, select your preferred flight, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, or EMI. Once payment is confirmed, your e-ticket will be sent to your registered email address within minutes.
  • Log in for faster checkout and loyalty points
  • Use filters to sort by price, duration, or airline
  • Add baggage or meals during checkout
How do I cancel my flight booking?
▼
Go to My Trips, find your booking, and click Cancel Booking. Cancellation charges depend on the airline's policy and how far in advance you cancel. Generally:
  • More than 7 days before departure: 90% refund
  • 3–7 days before departure: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days before departure: 10% refund
Refunds are processed within 3–7 business days to your original payment method. For fare-locked tickets, full refund is available within 24 hours of booking.
Can I change my flight date after booking?
▼
Yes, most airlines allow date changes subject to seat availability and applicable change fees. To reschedule:
  • Go to My Trips and click Modify Booking
  • Select a new date and confirm availability
  • Pay any fare difference and change fees if applicable
Date changes are not permitted within 12 hours of departure on most airlines. Some discounted fares are non-changeable — check your ticket's fare rules.
How do I complete web check-in for my flight?
▼
Web check-in is available from 48 hours to 1 hour before your flight's departure, depending on the airline. To check in:
  • Open your booking confirmation email or go to My Trips
  • Click Check-in Online — you'll be redirected to the airline's portal
  • Verify passenger details, select seats, and download your boarding pass
Web check-in is mandatory for international flights on most carriers. Make sure your passport details match your booking.
What is the baggage allowance for my flight?
▼
Baggage allowances vary by airline, route, and fare class. General guidelines:
  • Cabin baggage: 7–10 kg (1 piece, max 55×35×25 cm)
  • Check-in baggage (Economy): 15–25 kg depending on airline
  • Check-in baggage (Business): 30–40 kg typically
Your exact allowance is printed on your e-ticket. You can purchase additional baggage allowance through My Trips before departure at rates lower than airport counters.

Hotels

4 questions
What is the standard check-in time at hotels?
▼
Standard hotel check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is 12:00 PM (noon), though this varies by property. Early check-in (before 2 PM) and late check-out (after 12 PM) may be available on request, subject to room availability. Some hotels charge an additional fee. You can request early check-in through the hotel directly or via the My Trips section on TravelWise.
What is the hotel cancellation policy?
▼
Hotel cancellation policies depend on the rate type booked:
  • Free Cancellation rates: Full refund if cancelled more than 48 hours before check-in
  • Partially refundable: 50% refund if cancelled 24–48 hours before check-in
  • Non-refundable rates: No refund on cancellation
Always check the rate description before booking. The cancellation deadline is always shown in your booking confirmation.
Can I request a room upgrade?
▼
Room upgrades are subject to availability and the hotel's discretion. To request an upgrade:
  • Contact the hotel directly 24–48 hours before arrival
  • Use the Special Requests field during checkout for preferences
  • TravelWise Elite members often receive complimentary upgrades at partner hotels
Upgrades are not guaranteed and may involve an additional charge at the front desk.
How do I arrange for an extra bed?
▼
Extra beds or baby cots can be requested during the booking process using the Special Requests section. Availability and charges vary by hotel — most properties provide cots for infants at no charge, while extra beds for adults are typically charged at ₹500–₹2,000 per night. Confirm directly with the hotel after booking to ensure your request is noted.

Payments

4 questions
What payment methods are accepted on TravelWise?
▼
TravelWise accepts a wide range of payment options:
  • Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, RuPay (credit & debit)
  • UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, any UPI-enabled app
  • Net Banking: All major Indian banks
  • Wallets: Paytm, Amazon Pay, MobiKwik
  • EMI: Credit card EMI from 15+ banks (interest-free options available)
  • Buy Now Pay Later: LazyPay, Simpl
How can I pay in EMI? Which banks are supported?
▼
EMI is available on bookings above ₹5,000. To pay in EMI, select EMI as your payment method at checkout, choose your bank and tenure (3, 6, 9, or 12 months). Supported banks include:
  • HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis, Kotak, Yes Bank, IndusInd
  • No-cost EMI is available on select offers with certain banks
EMI is available on credit cards only. Check the offer details page for current no-cost EMI promotions.
My payment failed. What should I do?
▼
If your payment failed, here's what to do:
  • Check if money was deducted — if yes, wait 20–30 minutes for auto-reversal
  • Verify your card details, CVV, and billing address
  • Ensure your bank hasn't blocked the transaction (call your bank)
  • Try a different payment method or browser
  • Clear cache and cookies, then retry
If the amount was deducted but booking not confirmed, the money will be auto-refunded within 5–7 business days. Contact our support team with your transaction ID for faster resolution.
How do I get an invoice or GST receipt for my booking?
▼
Invoices are available from the My Trips section. Select your booking and click Download Invoice. For GST invoices with company GSTIN:
  • Enter your GSTIN during checkout in the billing section
  • Or update it in My Account → Billing Details within 7 days of booking
GST invoices for flights include IGST. Hotel invoices are issued by the property directly and are available in the booking confirmation email.

Refunds

4 questions
How long does a refund take to process?
▼
Refund timelines depend on your payment method:
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5–7 business days
  • UPI: 2–5 business days
  • Net Banking: 5–7 business days
  • Wallets: 1–3 business days
  • TravelWise Wallet: Instant
Processing begins after the airline/hotel confirms the cancellation. You'll receive an email and SMS when the refund is initiated.
How do I request a refund?
▼
Refunds are automatically initiated when you cancel a booking. To cancel and get a refund:
  • Go to My Trips and find your booking
  • Click Cancel Booking and review the refund amount
  • Confirm the cancellation — refund is automatically triggered
For airline-cancelled flights or hotel no-shows, you can raise a refund request directly via the Refunds page with your booking reference.
Why did I receive a partial refund?
▼
Partial refunds occur when cancellation charges apply. Common deductions include:
  • Airline cancellation fees (as per fare rules)
  • TravelWise convenience fee (non-refundable on most bookings)
  • Time-based deductions (less refund the closer to travel date)
  • GST on the cancelled portion
The exact refund breakdown is shown at the time of cancellation on the app/website before you confirm.
How do I track the status of my refund?
▼
You can track your refund status on the Refunds page using your booking reference number and registered email. Status updates include:
  • Requested: Cancellation confirmed, refund initiated with airline/hotel
  • Processing: Refund approved, being processed by payment gateway
  • Credited: Refund sent to your original payment method
You'll also receive email notifications at each stage.
