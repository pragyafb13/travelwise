Your privacy matters to us. TravelWise is committed to being transparent about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This policy applies to all services offered through travelwise.in and our mobile apps.
Section 01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you use TravelWise, as well as data generated automatically when you interact with our platform.

Personal Information — When you create an account, make a booking, or contact support, we may collect:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and nationality (required for flight bookings)
  • Passport or government-issued ID number (for international bookings)
  • Billing address and payment method details (processed securely via payment gateways — card numbers are never stored by TravelWise)
  • Emergency contact information (optional but recommended)

Travel Preferences — To personalize your experience, we collect and remember:

  • Preferred seat types, meal preferences, and cabin class
  • Destination wishlist and saved searches
  • Loyalty program numbers (frequent flyer, hotel rewards)
  • Communication and notification preferences

Device & Usage Data — Automatically collected when you use our platform:

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, search queries, and time spent on pages
  • Referring URLs and click-path data
  • Mobile device identifiers (when using our app)
  • Location data (only with your explicit permission)
Section 02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, always in line with applicable data protection laws:

  • Booking Processing — To confirm flights, hotels, and holiday packages, and to issue tickets and vouchers on your behalf
  • Personalization — To recommend destinations, show relevant deals, and tailor the TravelWise interface to your preferences
  • Communications — To send booking confirmations, check-in reminders, safety alerts, and service updates via email, SMS, or push notifications
  • Customer Support — To investigate issues, process refunds, and respond to your queries effectively
  • Security & Fraud Prevention — To detect and prevent fraudulent bookings, unauthorized account access, and abuse of our platform
  • Analytics & Improvement — To understand how users interact with TravelWise and improve our products and services
  • Legal Compliance — To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and government orders
Section 03

Information Sharing

We share your information only when necessary to deliver our services, and always with appropriate data protection safeguards in place.

  • Airlines & Hotels — We share your booking details (name, travel dates, preferences) with airlines, hotels, and service providers as required to fulfill your reservation
  • Payment Processors — Payment information is shared with PCI-DSS certified processors (Razorpay, Stripe) to complete transactions securely
  • Analytics Partners — We use aggregated, anonymized data with analytics providers (Google Analytics, Mixpanel) to understand platform usage patterns
  • Legal Requirements — We may disclose information in response to a valid legal request, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of TravelWise and its users
We never sell your personal data. TravelWise does not and will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing or advertising purposes.
Section 04

Data Security

TravelWise implements industry-leading security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • SSL Encryption — All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (256-bit encryption)
  • PCI-DSS Compliance — Our payment infrastructure maintains PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance — the highest standard in the industry
  • Regular Security Audits — We conduct quarterly penetration testing and annual third-party security audits of our systems
  • Access Controls — Employee access to personal data is strictly limited on a need-to-know basis with multi-factor authentication required
  • Data Minimization — We only collect and retain data that is necessary for the purposes described in this policy

While we take all reasonable precautions, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately at security@travelwise.in.

Section 05

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data held by TravelWise. To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@travelwise.in and we will respond within 30 days.

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Data Portability — Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format to transfer to another service
  • Opt-out of Marketing — Unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time via the link in any email, or through your Account Settings
  • Restrict Processing — Request that we limit how we use your data while a dispute is being resolved
  • Object to Processing — Object to processing of your data for direct marketing or profiling purposes
Section 06

Cookies

TravelWise uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide a personalized and seamless experience on our platform. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

We use essential cookies (necessary for the site to function), analytics cookies (to understand usage), marketing cookies (to show relevant offers), and preference cookies (to remember your settings).

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time. For full details on what cookies we use and how to control them, please see our Cookie Policy.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

TravelWise is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at privacy@travelwise.in and we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

Users between 13 and 18 years of age must have parental consent to use TravelWise and complete bookings.

Section 09

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons.

When we make material changes, we will notify you through one or more of the following methods:

  • Email notification to the address on your account (for significant changes)

Your continued use of TravelWise after the effective date of any revised Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
