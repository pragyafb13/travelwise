Our Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide and improve our services, and you can request its deletion at any time.

📋1. Information We Collect

When you use TravelWise, we collect information that is necessary to provide you with our travel booking services. This information falls into three main categories:

1.1 Personal Data You Provide

When you create an account, make a booking, or contact our support team, you may provide:

  • Full name, date of birth, and gender
  • Email address and phone number
  • Passport or government ID number (for international bookings)
  • Billing and shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely — we do not store full card numbers)
  • Travel preferences, meal preferences, and seat selections
  • Loyalty programme numbers for airlines and hotels

1.2 Usage Data (Automatically Collected)

When you visit our website or use our app, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate location (city/country level)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, search queries, and navigation patterns
  • Referral source (how you arrived at our site)
  • Session duration and interaction data

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your experience. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

⚙️2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

2.1 Providing Our Services

  • Processing flight, hotel, train, and holiday package bookings
  • Sending booking confirmations, e-tickets, and travel documents
  • Handling cancellations, changes, and refund requests
  • Providing customer support and resolving disputes
  • Processing payments and preventing fraud

2.2 Communications

  • Sending service-related notifications (flight delays, gate changes)
  • Marketing communications — only with your explicit consent
  • Price alerts and deals based on your search history (can be disabled)
  • Transactional emails related to your bookings
📧
Marketing Opt-out: You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the "Unsubscribe" link in any email, or by visiting Account Settings → Notifications.

2.3 Analytics & Improvement

We analyse aggregated, anonymised usage data to improve our platform, personalise search results, and develop new features. This analysis does not identify you personally.

🤝3. Sharing Your Information

🔒
We never sell your personal data. We only share your information with parties that are necessary to complete your booking or required by law.

3.1 Travel Partners

To complete your bookings, we share relevant personal data with:

  • Airlines, hotels, train operators, and holiday package providers
  • Car rental companies and transfer services
  • Travel insurance providers (if you opt for insurance)

These partners are bound by their own privacy policies and applicable data protection laws. They may only use your data to fulfil your booking.

3.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe, PayU) — for transaction processing
  • Cloud hosting providers (AWS, Google Cloud) — for infrastructure
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics) — for usage analysis
  • Customer support tools — for managing support tickets

All service providers operate under strict data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of TravelWise, our users, or the public. This includes sharing with government bodies, regulators, and law enforcement when legally obligated.

🔐4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (HTTPS)
  • Payment Security: Payment data is processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateways. We do not store full card numbers on our servers
  • Access Controls: Employee access to personal data is role-based, logged, and requires multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments
  • Data Minimisation: We only collect data that is necessary for our services
⚠️
Data Breach Notification: In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you and the relevant data protection authority within 72 hours of becoming aware, as required under applicable law.

⚖️5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have various rights regarding your personal data under laws including the GDPR (EU/UK) and India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDP):

5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.

5.2 Right to Rectification

You can update your personal information at any time through Account Settings. For booking-related data corrections, contact our support team.

5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account and personal data. Note that we may retain certain data for legal and accounting purposes for up to 7 years.

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) for transfer to another service provider.

5.5 Right to Object / Opt-out

You can opt out of marketing communications, analytics tracking, and personalised advertising at any time from Account Settings or by contacting us.

📬
Exercise Your Rights: Email privacy@travelwise.com with your request. Include your registered email and a description of your request. We respond within 30 days for non-complex requests.

🍪6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. We categorise our cookies as follows:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for site functionality (login session, cart). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site. You can opt out.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for personalised advertising. You can opt out.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings (theme, language). You can opt out.

For full details and cookie management options, see our Cookie Policy.

🔗7. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with various third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies and data practices:

👶8. Children's Privacy

TravelWise is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors without verifiable parental consent. Bookings for minor passengers require the account to be held by a parent or legal guardian aged 18 or above.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately at privacy@travelwise.com and we will delete the data promptly.
